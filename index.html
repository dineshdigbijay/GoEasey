About GoEasey

My Story

Hi, I’m Dinesh, the founder of GoEasey. As a former international student, I know how overwhelming Day 1 in Australia can be. I created GoEasey to help you feel at home, faster.

Coming from Nepal, I faced the same confusion and excitement as many new students. I wanted to make the journey easier for others by providing a kit that covers the real needs of students from Day 1. My motivation is to help you skip the stress, settle in quickly, and focus on your dreams. If I could do it, so can you—let’s make your Aussie adventure smooth and memorable!
